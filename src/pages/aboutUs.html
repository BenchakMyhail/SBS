Our story

Home / About us

The history of SBS company started in late 2009.

Since then, our company has achieved a lot. We have developed and worked on more than 1K various projects. We provide great variety of services: mobile development, static and responsive websites and single-page applications, portals/intranet portals, CMS, e-commerce solutions, dashboards, UI/UX for the web & mobile apps, games.

We’ve built close partnership with numerous clients all over the world.

Our relationship is based on trust and mutual respect.

Nowadays SBS company has nearly 100 employees. And we’re continuing to expand and to enlist talented developers into our family. So, if you need a transparent and clean code written by professionals, please fill in the request form on our Contact page and our managers will contact you.
